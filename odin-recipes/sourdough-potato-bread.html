Sourdough Potato Bread

Ingredients

  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 120g Milk
  • 75g Butter, softened
  • 50g Sugar
  • 22g Instant potato flakes with 118g of hot water
  • 1 egg
  • 10g salt
  • 440g Flour

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together until smooth dough forms
  2. Transfer dough to oiled bowl and cover, let rest until dough has doubled in size
  3. Shape the dough, cover and wait until the dough has double in size again
  4. Bake at 375F for 12-15 minutes
  5. Brush the freshly baked bread with butter while still warm
