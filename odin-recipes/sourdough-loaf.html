Sourdough Loaf

Ingredients

  • 50g Active Starter
  • 350g Water
  • 500g Flour
  • 10g Salt

Directions

  1. Mix together Starter and Water
  2. Add Flour and Salt to previous mix
  3. Continue mixing Ingredients until shaggy dough forms
  4. Do 4-6 sets of Fold and Turn
  5. Bulk ferment until doubled
  6. Shape dough and let sit for 30 minutes to 1 hour
  7. While dough is sitting, preheat your oven and dutch oven to 425 and boil a small pot of water
  8. Place dough on parchment paper and score
  9. Placed the boiled water on the bottom shelf of the oven and bake the dough in a covered dutch oven for 20 minutes
  10. Uncover the dough and bake for 25 more minutes
  11. Remove the dutch oven from the oven and lit sit in the dutch oven for 10 more minutes
  12. Remove the bread from the dutch oven and let cool until ready to serve.
