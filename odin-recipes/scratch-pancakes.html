Scratch Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour, (spooned and leveled)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 200F
  2. In a small bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  3. In a medium bowl, mix milk, butter and egg
  4. Add the flour mixture into the milk mixture and whisk until moistened
  5. Heat a large skillet over medium heat
  6. For each pancake, spoon 2-3 teaspoons of batter onto the skillet
  7. Cook for approximately 2 minutes until bubbles have formed and the underside is a shade of gold
  8. Flip with a thin spatula and cook for another 2 minutes, until the underside is browned
  9. Serve immediately, or keep warm in the preheated oven
